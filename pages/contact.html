Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I book a package?

Browse our packages, select your preferred dates, and click "Book Now". You can complete the booking online with a secure payment.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 30+ days before departure. 50% refund for 15-29 days. No refund within 14 days of departure.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely! Contact us and we'll work with you to create a personalized itinerary that fits your preferences and budget.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for bookings.

Do you provide travel guides?

Yes! All packages include professional local guides who speak English and are experts on their destinations.
